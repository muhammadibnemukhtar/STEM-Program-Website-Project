About Us:

The IEEE Joint Chapter Lahore Section is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting scientific research and technological advancements in Pakistan. We are a volunteer-run organization that is affiliated with the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), the world's largest professional organization for engineers and technologists.
Why following the IEEE Joint Chapter Lahore Section?

1) Access to our programs and services.
2) Networking opportunities.
3) Career development resources.
4) Supporting STEM education and outreach.

The IEEE Joint Chapter Lahore Section is looking forward with your quality and helpful suggestion.
